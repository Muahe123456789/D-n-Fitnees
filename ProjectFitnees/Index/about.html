ABOUT US


Who are we


"Fitness Point" is a leading fitness center, established in 2010.

With the mission of providing a professional and motivating environment to help people achieve their health and fitness goals. Since its inception, Fitness Point has grown steadily, expanding from a small gym to a chain of modern facilities nationwide. The center is equipped with advanced exercise equipment and offers a variety of classes, from yoga, pilates to high-intensity classes.

With a team of professional and dedicated trainers, Fitness Point is committed to providing the best training experience, helping members not only improve their health but also achieve mental and physical balance.

You’re on a journey to health and wellness. And we’re right there with you.

How you feel affects every single day of your life, which is why you work so hard to get well and stay well. No matter your journey, we’re here to support, guide, and inspire you.

We cut through the confusion with straightforward, expert-reviewed, person-first experiences — all designed to help you make the best decisions for yourself and the people you love.


Our Mission


Fitness Point's mission is to provide a professional and motivating workout environment that helps people of all ages and physical conditions achieve optimal health, fitness and well-being. We are committed to providing:

. Modern facilities with the most advanced equipment, to ensure comfort and safety during training.

. Comprehensive training programs from yoga, cardio, and high-intensity fitness classes to individually designed personal training sessions.

. Professional, dedicated and always accompanying coaches, motivating members to overcome challenges and achieve personal goals.
